Why convert PDF to JPG?

  • Editing: It's easier to crop extra white space from a JPG than a PDF.
  • Resizing: If you need to change the dimensions (e.g., width 500px), converting to image first is necessary.
  • Compatibility: Some older forms or specific fields (like Photo ID) may request JPG.

How to Convert PDF to Image

  1. Click "Upload PDF File" and choose your document.
  2. The tool processes the PDF and extracts each page.
  3. It renders them as high-definition JPG images.
  4. Download the specific page you need.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I upload JPG documents for UPSC?

UPSC OTR generally requires documents in PDF format. However, converting to JPG is useful if you need to crop, edit, or resize a document before converting it back to PDF for the final upload.

Does converting PDF to JPG lose quality?

Not with this tool. We use "High-Density Rendering" (Scale 2.0) to ensure that the converted image retains the sharpness of the original text and stamps.

Is it safe for sensitive certificates?

Yes. This converter operates 100% in your browser. Your degree, caste certificate, or marksheets are processed locally and never sent to any server.
