SSC Signature Guidelines

  • File Size: Official rule is 10-20KB. (This tool keeps high clarity under 50KB).
  • Dimensions: 4.0 cm width x 2.0 cm height.
  • Format: JPEG / JPG only.
  • Color: Black ink on white paper is preferred.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I upload a 50KB signature for SSC?

Technically, SSC asks for 10-20KB. If you want maximum clarity, use this tool to get a ~20-30KB file which is often accepted, or switch to our strict 20KB tool.

How to make a signature image clear?

Sign on plain white paper with a thick black pen. Scan or photograph it in bright light. This tool will then optimize the contrast and size.

Does this tool resize dimensions?

Yes, this tool automatically fixes the aspect ratio to 4:2 (Width:Height) which is the standard requirement for most government exams including SSC.
