Frequently Asked Questions

Why do I need to convert PDF to JPG for SSC?

The SSC recruitment portal often requires documents like marksheets and caste certificates to be uploaded in JPEG/JPG format only. PDF files are frequently rejected.

Does this tool maintain text clarity?

Yes. This tool renders your PDF at a high resolution (Scale 2.0) before converting it to an image, ensuring all text and stamps remain sharp and readable.

Is it safe to upload my certificates here?

Yes. The conversion happens entirely within your web browser using JavaScript. Your files are never uploaded to any external server.
