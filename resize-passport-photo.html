General Passport Photo Requirements

  • Dimensions: 3.5cm (width) x 4.5cm (height) is the most common global standard.
  • Format: JPG / JPEG format is universally accepted.
  • Background: Plain white or off-white background is recommended.
  • Quality: Clear, sharp, with full face visible facing the camera.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the standard dimensions for a passport photo?

The most common international standard dimensions for a passport size photograph are 3.5 cm (width) x 4.5 cm (height). This tool uses these dimensions automatically.

Can I convert a normal photo to passport size?

Yes, simply upload a clear photo (preferably with a plain background) and this tool will crop and resize it to the standard passport photo format.

How do I print this passport photo?

Download the resized image. You can place multiple copies of this image onto a standard 4x6 inch photo paper layout and print it using a color photo printer.

Is my photo uploaded to any server?

No. All processing happens locally in your web browser for maximum privacy. Your personal photos are never stored on our servers.
