Exam Document Guidelines

  • Semester Exams: Often require combining 10-20 pages of handwritten answers into one PDF file (Max 10MB).
  • Entrance Exams (JEE/NEET): Require single-page documents like Marksheets/Caste Certificates (Max 300KB).
  • Assignments: Should be clear and legible. Use "High Quality" mode.

Frequently Asked Questions

How to convert answer sheet photos to PDF?

Take photos of your answer pages in order. Select them all in this tool. It will combine them into a single PDF file suitable for uploading to your university portal.

What is the best size for uploading exam PDFs?

Most university portals allow up to 5MB or 10MB for answer scripts. For entrance exams (JEE/NEET) documents, the limit is usually smaller (200KB-500KB).

Does this tool maintain handwriting clarity?

Yes. We use a 'Standard' compression mode by default which reduces file size without blurring handwritten text, ensuring your answers are readable.
