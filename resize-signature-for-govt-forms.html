Common Signature Requirements (IBPS / SBI / SSC)

  • File Size: 10KB to 20KB is the industry standard.
  • Dimensions: 140x60 pixels (Banking) or 4x2 cm (SSC). This tool uses a high-density 2:1 ratio that works for both.
  • Format: JPG / JPEG.
  • Color: Black ink preferred on white paper.

How to Resize Signature for Online Forms

  1. Click "Upload Signature" and choose your file.
  2. The tool crops it to the standard rectangular (2:1) format.

  4. Download and upload to your application form.
