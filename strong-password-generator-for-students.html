Security Tips for Students

  • Passphrases: For Wi-Fi or computers you type into often, a phrase like `Pizza-Math-Book-24` is easier than `Xy9#mP2!`.
  • Random: For your main email or banking, stick to the Random mode.
  • Don't Share: Never share your portal password with friends or on public computers.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a good password for a student portal?

For student portals you use frequently, a 'Passphrase' (like 'Book-Coffee-Study-99') is best. It is long, secure against hacking, but easy for you to remember and type.

Is it safe to use this generator?

Yes, absolutely. This tool creates the password locally in your browser. Nothing is sent to our servers, ensuring your privacy.

Should I use the same password for all my classes?

No. If one account is compromised (e.g., a quiz site), hackers could access your main university email. Use unique passwords for every service.
