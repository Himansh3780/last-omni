Frequently Asked Questions

How do I make my Facebook password strong?

A strong Facebook password should be at least 12-14 characters long and include a complex mix of uppercase letters, numbers, and special symbols. Avoid common words.

Does Facebook allow special characters in passwords?

Yes, using special characters like @, #, $, and & is highly recommended on Facebook to prevent dictionary attacks.

Is it safe to use this generator?
