UPSC Photo Guidelines (Why 50KB?)

  • Official Limit: 20KB to 300KB.
  • Recommended: 50KB (Balances quality and small file size).
  • Dimensions: 350px width x 350px height.
  • Content: Face should occupy 75% of the area.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why resize UPSC photo to 50KB?

While UPSC allows up to 300KB, a 50KB file is the sweet spot: it is small enough for instant upload even on slow networks, but large enough to keep the face clear.

Does this tool create a square photo?

Yes, this tool automatically resizes your image to 350x350 pixels (Square format) as required by UPSC notification.

Is 50KB valid for UPSC?

Yes, the official range is 20KB to 300KB. A 50KB file is perfectly valid and safe to use.
