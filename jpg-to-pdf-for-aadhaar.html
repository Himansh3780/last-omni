Document Upload Guidelines (UIDAI)

  • Format: PDF (Preferred for multiple pages) or JPEG/PNG.
  • Size: Max 2 MB.
  • Content: Must show both sides of the document if address/details are on the back (e.g., Voter ID, DL).
  • Clarity: Details like Name and Address must be readable.

How to Create PDF for Aadhaar Update

  1. Click "Select Front & Back Photos".
  2. Choose the images of your document (e.g., Front side and Back side).
  3. The tool combines them onto separate pages in one PDF file.
  4. It automatically optimizes the size to be under 2MB. Download and upload.

Frequently Asked Questions

How to combine front and back photos for Aadhaar upload?

Select both the front and back photos of your document in this tool. It will merge them into a single PDF file which is required for the UIDAI portal.

What is the maximum file size for Aadhaar PDF?

The maximum allowed file size for document upload on the myAadhaar portal is 2 MB. This tool automatically ensures your PDF stays within this limit.

Is it safe to upload my ID proofs here?

Yes. This tool works entirely in your browser (Client-Side). Your photos and the generated PDF are processed locally and never sent to any server.
