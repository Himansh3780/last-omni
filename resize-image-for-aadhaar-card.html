Aadhaar Upload Requirements (UIDAI)

  • Document Size: Max 2 MB (for POA/POI updates).
  • Photo Size: Standard Passport Size (for enrollment forms).
  • Formats: JPEG, PNG, or PDF.
  • Clarity: Scanned documents must be legible and clear.

How to Resize for Aadhaar Update

  1. Click "Upload Photo/Document" and pick your file.
  2. The tool optimizes the file to ensure it's under the size limit.
  3. It converts the file to a standard JPG format compatible with UIDAI.
  4. Download and upload to the myAadhaar portal.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the image size for Aadhaar upload?

For the myAadhaar portal (POI/POA documents), the file size must be less than 2 MB. For enrollment photos, standard passport size is used.

Can I resize Aadhaar document on mobile?

Yes, simply select your scanned document or photo in this tool, and it will optimize it for UIDAI upload instantly.

Is JPEG allowed for Aadhaar upload?

Yes, UIDAI accepts JPEG, PNG, and PDF formats for document uploads. This tool outputs optimized JPEG files.

Is my Aadhaar data safe?

Yes. This tool processes your images locally in your browser. No documents are ever uploaded to our servers.
