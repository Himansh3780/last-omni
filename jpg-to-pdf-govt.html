Common Document Limits

  • UPSC / SSC: Often require PDFs between 20KB and 300KB.
  • Bank / IBPS: Typically allow 200KB to 500KB.
  • Aadhaar / Passport: Usually allow up to 2MB.
  • Tip: Choose "Small Size" for SSC/UPSC and "Standard" for Aadhaar.

Frequently Asked Questions

How to convert multiple JPGs to one PDF?

Select all your images (marksheets, certificates) in this tool. It automatically arranges them on A4 pages and combines them into a single PDF file ready for upload.

Can I reduce the PDF size to under 500KB?

Yes. Select the "Small (Under 500KB)" option before converting. The tool will apply compression to ensure your final PDF meets the strict size limits of government portals.

Is it safe for Aadhaar and PAN cards?

Yes. This tool uses "Client-Side" processing. Your images and the generated PDF are processed entirely within your browser and are never uploaded to any server.
