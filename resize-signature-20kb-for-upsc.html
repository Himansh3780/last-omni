Frequently Asked Questions

What is the signature size for UPSC?

UPSC accepts signature files between 20KB and 300KB. This tool targets the 20KB range for fast, efficient uploads.

What are the pixel dimensions for UPSC signature?

The preferred dimension is 350 pixels (width) by 150 pixels (height) to ensure the signature fits perfectly in the application form box.

Can I use a blue ink signature?

UPSC usually recommends a BLACK ink pen on white paper for the best clarity and contrast, but blue is often accepted if scanned clearly.
